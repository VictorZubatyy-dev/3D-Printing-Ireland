About Us.

Our goal here at 3D-Printing Ireland provide people the with the best equipment for their creative imagination. Our company startup took place in early 2021. We recieved major investments from Enterprise Ireland, they believed in our objective and goals that we wanted to achieve. Due to the COVID-19 pandemic, our main goal have been shifted to the saftey and protection of elderly people. We have redirected most of our resources into creating 3D-Printed saftey equipment such as face shields and sneeze guards.
